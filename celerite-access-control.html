Key Features

User and Role Analysis

Dive deep into your SAP access structure to identify current user roles, responsibilities, and permissions. Gain insights into user activities and pinpoint security gaps.
Role Security Analysis

Assess the robustness of your role structures. Identify potential vulnerabilities, such as excessive permissions or conflicting roles, that could compromise system security.
User and Role Carveout

Extract specific user and role data subsets for targeted analysis, testing, or migration purposes.
User and Role Decommissioning

Safely and effectively remove obsolete or unnecessary users and roles from the system, ensuring clean and efficient access control.
User and Role Remediation

Address identified issues, filling security gaps, and correcting misconfigured permissions.
User and Role Comparison

Compare users and roles across different systems, clients, or periods. Highlight differences in permissions, attributes, and configurations.
Role Standardization

Define and implement standard naming conventions, permissions, and role structures, ensuring consistency and clarity across the SAP environment.
User and Role Transformation

Adjust and adapt user roles and permissions as per changing business requirements, without compromising security.
User and Role Migration

Seamlessly move users and roles between different SAP systems or versions, ensuring data integrity and minimizing operational disruptions.
User and Role Optimization

Evaluate and fine-tune user roles and permissions for optimal efficiency, security, and compliance.
